% ========================== RESEARCH OVERVIEW ==========================
\section*{Research Overview}
My research is in embodied AI and robot learning: building agents that perceive, reason about, and act in the physical world, and that learn to collaborate with people. Topics I worked on in the past include:
\begin{itemize}
    \item[$\circ$] \textbf{\textcolor{jhublue}{Long-horizon, physically grounded agents.}}
    \item[$\circ$] \textbf{\textcolor{jhublue}{Human-robot collaboration}}, including inferring human intent and grounding natural language into executable, physically grounded plans.
    \item[$\circ$] \textbf{\textcolor{jhublue}{Multimodal theory of mind and social reasoning}} in multi-agent settings.
    \item[$\circ$] \textbf{\textcolor{jhublue}{Generative world models}} for embodied belief inference, planning, and perception under occlusion.
\end{itemize}
